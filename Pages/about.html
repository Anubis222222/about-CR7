Early Life

Cristiano Ronaldo was born on February 5, 1985, in Madeira, Portugal. He began playing football at a young age and quickly rose through the ranks.

Education and Career

He joined Sporting CP's youth academy before moving to Manchester United at the age of 18. Later, he played for Real Madrid, Juventus, and returned to Manchester United before joining Al Nassr in Saudi Arabia.
